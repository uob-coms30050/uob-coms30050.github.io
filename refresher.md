# Python Refresher

The [Jean Golding Institute (JGI)](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/golding/) at the University of Bristol offers excellent training materials and resources on data science and engineering. If your Python knowledge is a bit rusty, start with the beginner and intermediate resources below. You can explore the advanced topics later in the term.

## Resources

- **Introduction to Python**  `Beginner`  
  - [Part 1](https://bristol-training.github.io/intro-python-1/)
  - [Part 2](https://bristol-training.github.io/intro-python-2/)

- **Introduction to Data Analysis in Python**  `Intermediate`  
  - [Part 1](https://bristol-training.github.io/data-analysis-python-1/)
  - [Part 2](https://bristol-training.github.io/data-analysis-python-2/)

- **[Introduction to NumPy](https://milliams.com/courses/intro_numpy/)**  `Intermediate`  

- **[Parallel Python](https://milliams.com/courses/parallel_python/)**  `Advanced`  
 
- **[Accelerating Python](https://chryswoods.com/accelerating_python/)**  `Advanced`
